About ApnaGhar

"A home filled with love, care, and hope for a brighter future."

Who We Are

ApnaGhar is an orphanage located on the outskirts of the city, offering shelter, education, and emotional support to children who have lost their families. Our mission is to create a nurturing environment where every child feels safe, loved, and empowered to achieve their dreams.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to provide abandoned and orphaned children with education, healthcare, and a loving home. We envision a world where every child grows up with equal opportunities and the chance to lead a successful life.

Our History

Founded over a decade ago, ApnaGhar has been a sanctuary for children in need. From humble beginnings to becoming a well-established home, we have supported hundreds of children in their journey toward a better future. Our dedicated team of social workers, teachers, and volunteers work tirelessly to provide them with a safe and supportive environment.
